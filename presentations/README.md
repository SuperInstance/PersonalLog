## 🎤 Presentation Tips

### Before the Talk

1. **Practice 10+ Times** - Rehearse until it's natural
2. **Test Demos** - Run through demos multiple times
3. **Backup Plans** - Have video demos if live fails
4. **Know Your Audience** - Tailor content to attendees
5. **Check Setup** - Verify A/V equipment beforehand

### During the Talk

1. **Start Strong** - Hook audience in first 2 minutes
2. **Pacing** - Aim for 5 min under time limit
3. **Engage** - Ask questions, take polls
4. **Demos** - Explain what you're doing
5. **Q&A** - Repeat questions for audience

### After the Talk

1. **Share Slides** - Upload to SlideShare/LinkedIn
2. **Distribute One-Pagers** - Hand out or email
3. **Collect Feedback** - Send survey link
4. **Follow Up** - Connect on social media
5. **Blog Post** - Write about the experience
